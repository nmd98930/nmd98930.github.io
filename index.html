Man's Best Friend

For my Wild Card, I decided to use the website I created during this semester instead of a typical writing piece. In "The Doggie World of Nautas," I wrote about my four dogs, and using some simple HTML tags, I was able to format it like a real website that anyone can access with the hyperlink. Because I am not artistically gifted, programming or coding allowed me to go outside the box for my Wild Card. In making this website, I was introduced into how to use the basics of HTML, and this simple website may lead to future endeavors in coding. Furthermore, this allowed me to write on a topic I adore with all my heart, dogs. For centuries, these animals have proven to be faithful companions to humans, and as a result, they have become "man's best friend." In many creative pieces, dogs are featured prominently such as Argos in the Odyssey and have even been the muse of artists such as Cassius Coolidge's Dogs Playing Poker, showing how much impact they have on our lives. Similar to writers, dogs continue to give us entertainment and joy, and I want to show this message through my website.
